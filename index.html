Elio is my latest obsession—a cross-platform app for Android & iOS designed to help Elio California reward their regulars. I'm building it from the ground up, handling everything from sleek animations to the payment logic. It's still in the oven, but I'm getting it ready for the app stores soon!

  • Apple & Google Wallet with dynamic pass updates
  • Stripe payments with real transaction processing
  • Sign in with Apple & Google authentication
